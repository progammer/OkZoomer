Info

Welcome to OK Zoomer! To get started, click on [Add Zoom ] or [Add Link ] to add a meeting to your dashboard.

[Add Zoom ] is built to support Zoom Meetings (which are described by unique meeting IDs). Enter your meeting ID and submit to save it.

[Add Link ] should be used for any other platforms (such as Microsoft Teams or Blackboard Collaborate). Simply enter a valid link that gives access to the desired meeting. Also, it is recommended that you name the meeting in the case that the link alone is nondescript.

Once a meeting has been added to the dashboard, you can join it [big blue button], remove it [], edit its properties [], or modify its name by selecting its containing field (unfocus to save).

Within the edit field (accessible via a meeting's edit button []), you can schedule meeting times by using the selector for a day/time and saving with [Save Time]. You can also specify passwords for Zoom meetings and enable reminders/autojoin with their respective switches.
